Exam preparation TOPIK/EPS TOPIK
Sign up

TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) is a state exam to certify the level of Korean language proficiency to persons who are not native speaker of Korean.

EPS-TOPIK is an employment-related Korean language test and is therefore administered by the Ministry of Employment and Labor. In short, EPS-TOPIK is a required Korean language test for working in Korea as a foreigner.

Frequent questions

How to enroll to study in Korea?

More

To go to study in Korea, you need to study Korean or English. After learning the language, we collect documents, apply for study at universities.

Is it possible to enroll without knowledge of the language?

More

To go to study in Korea, you need to know Korean. The level of knowledge of the Korean language (TOPIK) should be 2 or higher.

Is it possible to enroll after the 9th grade?

More

For admission , you need a certificate of the 11th grade or a university diploma.

Are there any age restrictions?

More

The age limit is from 17 to 26 years.
